Community Rules

WhatsNaija is a place for everyone. These rules exist to keep the platform safe, respectful, and useful. All users are expected to follow them. Violations may result in content removal, warnings, or account suspension.

Safety
No Threats of Violence: Do not threaten, encourage, or glorify violence against any individual or group. This includes direct threats, veiled threats, and calls to action.
No Harassment or Bullying: Do not target individuals with repeated unwanted contact, personal attacks, intimidation, or coordinated abuse. This includes doxxing (sharing someone's private information).
No Hate Speech: Content that promotes hatred, discrimination, or dehumanization based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or nationality is not allowed.
Content Integrity
No Spam: Do not post repetitive, misleading, or low-effort content designed to manipulate visibility. This includes excessive self-promotion, link farming, and bot-generated content.
No Impersonation: Do not pretend to be another person, brand, or organization. Parody accounts must be clearly labeled as such.
No Misinformation: Do not knowingly share false or misleading information, especially on topics related to health, safety, or current events.
No Copyright Violations: Do not post content you do not own or have the right to share. This includes images, articles, videos, and other copyrighted material. Always credit original creators.
Legal & Privacy
No Illegal Content: Do not post content that promotes, facilitates, or depicts illegal activity. This includes drug trafficking, fraud, and other criminal conduct.
Respect Privacy: Do not share personal or private information about others without their explicit consent. This includes real names, addresses, phone numbers, workplaces, and photos.
No NSFW or Explicit Content: Sexually explicit material, graphic violence, and other content not suitable for general audiences is not permitted.
Community Standards
Stay On Topic: Post content relevant to the community you're in. Off-topic posts may be removed.
Use Descriptive Titles: Write clear, accurate titles so others can find and understand your posts.
Be Constructive: Disagree respectfully. Critique ideas, not people. Add value to discussions rather than tearing them down.
Report, Don't Retaliate: If you see a rule violation, use the report feature. Do not engage or escalate. Moderators will handle it.
Enforcement: Moderators may remove content, issue warnings, or suspend accounts at their discretion. Repeated or severe violations may result in a permanent ban. If you believe an action was taken in error, you can reach out to the moderation team. For full legal terms, see our Terms of Service.
