Frequently Asked Questions
Can I pay with my Nigerian debit card?
Yes! The Paystack option accepts all Nigerian bank cards — Verve, Visa, and Mastercard. Your card is charged in Naira, so there are no foreign exchange surprises.
I don't have internet banking. Can I still donate?
Absolutely. Use USSD — for example GTBank (*737#), Access Bank (*901#), or Zenith Bank (*966#) — to send money directly from your phone without needing mobile data or internet. Instructions are in the Bank Transfer tab.
Will I get a receipt or confirmation?
Paystack sends an automatic email receipt. For bank transfers, you'll get a debit alert from your bank. Feel free to share your transfer screenshot with us — we'll give you a shoutout if you like!
Can I donate anonymously?
Yes. You don't need a WhatsNaija account to donate, and we will never publicly share donor information without your explicit consent.
Why is USDT (TRC-20) recommended for crypto?
USDT on the Tron network (TRC-20) has very low transaction fees (often under $1) and fast confirmations — making it one of the most practical crypto options for Nigerians who already hold stablecoins.
